About Me

My name is Michelle Ranalli. I was born and raised in Mexico. I am Mexican from my mother's side and Italian from my father's side. I came to America when I was eighteen years old, and enrolled in an adult school to learn English. After graduating in a year, I went to East Los Angeles College, then transferred to Azusa Pacific University. I currently hold a BM in Music Performance from Azusa Pacific University- Summa Cum Laude December 2018.

I have worked a myriad of jobs that range from music instructor to flight attendant. Months later I saw that the bootcamp by Dr Angela Yu was on sale, and I decided to start all over. I am passionate about learning technology, and I am proud of myself for achieving this dream.
